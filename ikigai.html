Ikigai: The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life

Author: Francesc Miralles and Hector Garcia

Published: 2016

Description:

Overview :

*Ikigai: Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life* by Héctor García and Francesc Miralles explores the Japanese philosophy of *ikigai*, which means "a reason for being." The book suggests that discovering one's ikigai can lead to a more meaningful, happy, and long life. It explores how ikigai lies at the intersection of four elements:

1. **What you love** (your passion),

2. **What you are good at** (your vocation),

3. **What the world needs** (your mission),

4. **What you can be paid for** (your profession).

Through interviews with residents of Okinawa, Japan—known for its high percentage of centenarians—the authors identify lifestyle practices that contribute to health and longevity, like a balanced diet, regular physical activity, strong social ties, and a positive mindset. Key insights emphasize the importance of small, joyful routines, resilience, and staying active. *Ikigai* is a mix of self-help advice, Japanese philosophy, and lifestyle tips aimed at guiding readers toward a life filled with purpose, balance, and fulfillment.
